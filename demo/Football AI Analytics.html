Football AI Analytics Help

Getting Started

Upload a football match video or use the demo to analyze player positions, team formations, ball possession, and more.

Analysis Features

  • Player Tracking: View the real-time positions of players, goalkeepers, and referees on the field with team identification.
  • Team Control: Visualize the areas of the pitch controlled by each team using Voronoi diagrams.
  • Ball Tracking: Follow the ball's movement patterns and speed throughout the match.
  • Statistics: View detailed stats about possession, territory, player speed, and distances covered.

Technologies Used

This application uses computer vision and artificial intelligence to track players, determine which team they belong to, and calculate various metrics. The system is powered by object detection, keypoint detection, and sophisticated tracking algorithms.
